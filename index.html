Blood Donation Awareness & Tips

Pre-Donation Tips

  • Drink plenty of water before donating.
  • Eat a healthy meal, avoid fatty foods.
  • Get a good night's sleep.
  • Bring your ID and a list of medications you take.

Post-Donation Care

  • Keep the bandage on for the next few hours.
  • Rest for a few minutes before leaving the center.
  • Avoid strenuous physical activity for 24 hours.
  • Drink extra fluids for the next day.
